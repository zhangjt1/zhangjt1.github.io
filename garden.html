An interactive p5.js visualization exploring fluid dynamics and particle systems. Watch waves flow and interact with your cursor.

🎵 Note Calculator

Convert frequencies to musical notes with cents deviation. Visualize notes in standard musical notation using Verovio. Supports both sharp and flat accidentals.

🎛️ MIDI Granulator

A Google Colab notebook that applies a granulator to MIDI files, using PrettyMIDI.
